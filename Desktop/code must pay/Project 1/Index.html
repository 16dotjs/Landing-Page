What We Offer

Our Medical
Departments

Specialised care across every discipline — delivered by experienced consultants using the latest medical technology.

Cardiology

Advanced heart care including diagnostics, interventional procedures, and long-term cardiac management by specialist cardiologists.

Neurology

Comprehensive diagnosis and treatment of nervous system conditions including stroke, epilepsy, and neurodegenerative disorders.

General Surgery

Minimally invasive and open surgical procedures performed by our board-certified surgeons in state-of-the-art operating theatres.

Paediatrics

Dedicated child-friendly wards and clinics staffed by paediatricians who specialise in neonatal to adolescent healthcare.

Radiology & Imaging

High-resolution MRI, CT, X-ray, and ultrasound imaging with rapid reporting by consultant radiologists.

Laboratory Services

Accredited diagnostic laboratory offering comprehensive blood panels, pathology, microbiology, and genetic testing.
