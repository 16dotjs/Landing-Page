FAQs

Questions we
actually get asked

Can I switch plans at any time?
Yes, absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time from your settings. Upgrades are prorated. Downgrades take effect at end of your billing cycle.
Is there a free trial for paid plans?
Yes. Pro comes with a 14-day free trial, no credit card required. You get full access to all Pro features so you can evaluate before committing.
How does billing work for teams?
Billing is per active seat per month. You're only charged for team members who are active in a given billing period. Add or remove seats anytime.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation. You can export everything in that window. After 30 days, all data is permanently deleted from our servers.

Our Mission

We believe software should get out of your way

In 2021, our founding team was running a remote product agency. We were juggling Jira, Notion, Slack, and spreadsheets — spending more time managing tools than actually shipping work.

We built Flowdesk in three weeks as an internal experiment. Within a month, three other teams asked to use it. That was enough of a signal.

Today, Flowdesk is the single source of truth for engineering, design, and ops teams at companies from 5 to 5,000 people.
