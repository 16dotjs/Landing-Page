FAQs

Questions we
actually get asked

Can I switch plans at any time?
Yes, absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time from your settings. Upgrades are prorated. Downgrades take effect at end of your billing cycle.
Is there a free trial for paid plans?
Every paid plan comes with a 14-day free trial, no credit card required. Full access to all features. After 14 days, choose to subscribe or downgrade to free.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
Data stays in read-only mode for 30 days. Export everything within that window. After 30 days, data is permanently deleted.
Do you offer discounts for nonprofits?
Yes — 50% off for registered nonprofits and educational institutions. Reach out via Contact with proof of status.
Is Flowdesk secure?
All data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR compliant, hosted on AWS.

Our Mission

We believe software should get out of your way

In 2021, our founding team was running a remote product agency. We were juggling Jira, Notion, Slack, and spreadsheets — spending more time managing tools than actually shipping work.

We built Flowdesk in three weeks as an internal experiment. Within a month, three other teams asked to use it. That was enough of a signal.

Today, Flowdesk is the single source of truth for engineering, design, and ops teams at companies from 5 to 5,000 people. Our mission hasn't changed: software that helps you think less about process and more about work.
